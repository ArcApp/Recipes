Transforming Furikake Gohan

Serves 2-3 people

Ingredients:

  • 7 Chicken Wings
  • 1 Tbsp Sesame Oil
  • Group A:
    • 1 Tsp Grated Ginger
    • 700cc Bonito Broth
    • 1.5 Tbsp Sake
    • 1.5 Tbsp Sugar
    • 1.5 Tbsp Mirin
  • 4 Eggs
  • Minced Spring Onion
  • Group B:
    • 1 Tbsp sugar
    • 1 Pinch salt
  • Hot Steamed Rice

Directions:

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a frying pan and fry the chicken wings until they are browned.
  2. Put the chicken wings and the ingredients in Group A in a pot
  3. Put pot on high flame. When the contents boil, skim the scum from the broth.
  4. Put the broth in a tray, let it cool down and then refrigerate it.
  5. Take the chicken wings' bones out and cut the meat finely.
  6. Scramble eggs in a bowl and mix them with the ingredients in Group B.
  7. Put the mix in a frying pan on low flame and stir it quickly until the eggs are finely minced.
  8. When the refrigerated broth hardens, dice it, making the cubes about 1cm long.
  9. Put the jellied meat broth, the scrambled eggs, the chicken wings, and minced spring onion on hot rice
  10. VOILA!!!! Time to enjoy!
